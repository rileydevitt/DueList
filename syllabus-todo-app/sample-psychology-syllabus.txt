Course Objectives:
Students will learn fundamental research methodologies used in psychological science, including experimental design, data collection techniques, statistical analysis, and research ethics.

ASSIGNMENT SCHEDULE:

Research Ethics Training Completion
Due: January 18, 2024
Complete online CITI training modules and submit completion certificate.

Literature Review Assignment
Due: February 1, 2024
Write a 5-page literature review on an approved research topic in cognitive psychology.

Hypothesis Development Workshop
Date: February 8, 2024
In-class workshop to develop testable hypotheses for your research proposal.

Research Proposal Draft
Due: February 22, 2024
Submit 8-page research proposal including literature review, methodology, and expected outcomes.

Peer Review Session
Date: March 1, 2024
Exchange proposals with classmates for constructive feedback and suggestions.

Statistics Exam #1
Date: March 8, 2024
Covers descriptive statistics, probability, and hypothesis testing fundamentals.

Revised Research Proposal
Due: March 22, 2024
Final version of research proposal incorporating peer feedback and instructor comments.

Data Collection Period
March 25 - April 12, 2024
Conduct your approved research study and collect data from participants.

Preliminary Data Analysis
Due: April 15, 2024
Submit initial statistical analysis of your collected data with interpretations.

Statistics Exam #2
Date: April 19, 2024
Advanced statistics including ANOVA, regression, and non-parametric tests.

Research Presentation
Date: April 26, 2024
15-minute presentation of your complete research study including methodology, results, and implications.

Final Research Report
Due: May 3, 2024
Complete APA-style research paper (12-15 pages) documenting your study from conception to conclusion.

Final Comprehensive Exam
Date: May 10, 2024, 10:30 AM
Cumulative exam covering all research methods, statistics, and ethical considerations.
