Course Description:
This course examines digital marketing strategies in the modern business environment. Students will learn to develop comprehensive digital marketing campaigns, analyze consumer behavior online, and measure marketing effectiveness using analytics tools.

COURSE SCHEDULE AND DELIVERABLES:

Week 1-2: Digital Marketing Foundations
Reading Assignment: Chapters 1-3 of textbook
Due: September 8, 2024

Case Study Analysis #1: Netflix Marketing Evolution
Due: September 15, 2024
Individual assignment analyzing Netflix's digital marketing transformation over the past decade.

Group Formation and Topic Selection
Due: September 22, 2024
Form teams of 4-5 students and select final project topic from approved list.

Market Research Proposal
Due: October 6, 2024
3-page proposal outlining your team's research methodology for target market analysis.

Midterm Examination
Date: October 20, 2024, 6:00 PM
Covers: Digital marketing fundamentals, consumer psychology, and platform strategies.

Social Media Campaign Draft
Due: November 3, 2024
Present preliminary social media campaign strategy including platform selection and content calendar.

Case Study Analysis #2: Failed Digital Campaigns
Due: November 17, 2024
Analyze a major digital marketing failure and propose alternative strategies.

Final Project Presentation
Date: December 1, 2024
20-minute team presentation of complete digital marketing campaign with implementation timeline.

Peer Evaluation Forms
Due: December 1, 2024
Anonymous evaluation of team member contributions throughout semester.

Final Marketing Campaign Report
Due: December 8, 2024
Comprehensive 15-page report detailing your team's digital marketing strategy including budget analysis and ROI projections.

Final Exam
Date: December 15, 2024, 2:00 PM
Location: Business Building Auditorium
Focus on campaign analysis, metrics interpretation, and strategic recommendations.
